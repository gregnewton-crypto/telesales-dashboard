The two ideas, side by side

🅰️ Split by team

4 separate bases, one per team
  • ❌ Breaks the call ↔ lead links
  • ❌ Calls keep growing — not solved
  • ❌ Dashboard rollups break
  • ✅ Simple to picture
Verdict: Not recommended

🅱️ Split by temperature

Live · Reporting · Archive (per year)
  • ✅ Links stay intact (all in Live)
  • ✅ Calls are bounded for good
  • ✅ Reporting stays tiny
  • ⚠️ Needs a careful archive routine
Verdict: Recommended ✔

Making archiving bulletproof

🔑 Golden rule: never delete anything until a verified copy exists in the archive.
