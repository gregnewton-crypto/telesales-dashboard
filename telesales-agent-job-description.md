# Telesales Agent — Butternut Box

**Hybrid (office + home) · Flexible shifts · Competitive hourly rate + uncapped commission**

> **Fresh Food, Happy Dogs.**

We believe dogs deserve the healthiest, most natural and tastiest meals — with none of the nasties. That's why we make FRESHly prepared food, cooked just like you would at home and delivered direct to our customers in perfect portions. As a B-Corp certified company, you're joining a sustainable business that genuinely cares about our people, our products and our impact on the planet. Our goal is simple: to help dogs live healthier, happier and longer lives, while leaving humans smiling after every interaction.

We've come a long way since our co-founders (Kev & Dave) hand-delivered the very first Butternut Box. ## ☎️ The job

Looking for flexible hours, brilliant earnings and a product you'll genuinely love selling? As a **Telesales Agent**, you'll earn a competitive hourly rate **plus uncapped commission** while helping pet pawrents make the switch to fresh food that helps their dogs thrive.

You'll be a founding member of our growing telesales channel — calling **warm leads** (owners who have already told us they're interested in Butternut Box), building trust through great conversations, and turning that interest into happy new customers. No cold calling, no scripts to read robotically — just real conversations about something every dog lover cares about.

This is easy selling with a strong product and a supportive, ambitious team behind you. The best part? **Flexible shifts** let you build a high-earning career while keeping time for the rest of your life.

## 🦴 What your role entails

- Call warm leads and help pet owners make the switch to fresh food.
- Build rapport quickly, ask the right questions, and recommend the right plan for each dog.
- Confidently handle questions and objections, and guide interested owners to their first box.
- Keep your pipeline, notes and call outcomes accurate and up to date in our CRM.
- Hit (and beat!) your conversion and sales targets.
- Listen back to calls with your team lead, take on feedback, and apply it straight away.
- Help our telesales channel grow and perform better and better — your ideas count.

## ☀️ A day in the life of a Butternut Box Telesales Agent

You log in, put on your headset, and your leads are ready: owners who have personally expressed interest in Butternut Box. After a quick check-in with your team lead, you get to work.

Your first conversation is with the owner of a dog that reacts poorly to dry food. You ask the right questions, build trust and give tailored advice. You hit it off, and they start with their first box. You can already tell this customer is a perfect fit — and that it won't stop at one box.

Halfway through your shift, you listen back to a call with your team lead. You get targeted feedback and apply it immediately, and your conversations run even more smoothly afterwards.

At the end of your shift, you can see exactly what you've achieved: four new clients and four dogs that will truly benefit from Butternut Box. At the end of the month, you'll see it on your payslip too — including commission for every new customer. Kaching. 🐾
