D2MS Retention Data Plan

Stage 1 plan for measuring retention, pause behaviour, and early churn risk.

Simple Aim

Who stays? Measure 28-day and 84-day retention.
Who pauses? Track customers who pause before box 1 arrives.
What changes? Turn findings into clear D2MS actions.

Key Measures

  • 28 Day Retention
  • 84 Day Retention
  • Paused Before First Box
  • Cancellation Reason
  • Payment Failure
  • Agent Performance

Paused Before First Box Formula

Use this if the sheet has Order 1 Delivery Date and Pause Date.

=IF(AND(PauseDate<>"",Order1DeliveryDate<>"",PauseDate<Order1DeliveryDate),"Yes","No")
